Get set up in minutes

Follow these quick steps to start generating n8n JSON.

Step 1

Add your n8n domain in Settings and click Request Access Permission.

Step 2

In API & Integrations, enable your AI provider and add its API key.

Step 3

Ensure the Generate button is enabled.

Step 4

On your n8n workflow page, click Generate, enter your query, select the ai provide and hit Generate. Happy Generation!

Please enable and configure at least one AI provider in the Settings tab.
